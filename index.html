What We Do

Administrative support that actually moves the needle

Every service is performed by Amanda Bosley, LPN — with hands-on experience in both clinical and administrative healthcare settings. You're not getting a generalist virtual assistant. You're getting someone who has lived this work.

01
Prior Authorization Management
End-to-end PA handling across all major payers — submission, tracking, peer-to-peer coordination, and appeals. Stop losing revenue to preventable denials.
02
Referral Management
Complete referral processing from submission through confirmation and closed-loop documentation. Every referral tracked, every gap closed.
03
Denial Management
Root cause analysis, appeal preparation, and resubmission to recover revenue you've already earned. Most practices leave thousands on the table.
04
Insurance Follow-Up
AR follow-up, EOB review, and persistent payer communication to move stuck claims through the system and into your account.
05
Backlog Cleanup
One-time audit and cleanup of outstanding PAs, referrals, and unpaid claims. A great low-risk way to start — see the results before committing to anything ongoing.
06
Remote Medical Scribing
Real-time or async chart documentation support. Give your providers back the time they spend on notes and reduce after-hours charting burnout.
